About

IMF world economic data from December 2016. Choose any of the available statistics and up to 20 countries. Countries can be added or removed individually or a group of countries can be selected either from a geographic (e.g. Western Europe) or an other grouping (e.g. G8). It is also possible to select 10 countries with the lowest or highest statistical values within the selected time period. The start and end years are adjustable with minimum of 5 years being displayed. The original data is available from IMF.
